Tuna Steaks with Mushroom Sauce and Cuscus

This recipe will delight anyone who tries it.

Ingredients

  • 4 tuna steaks
  • 2 tbsp. of butter
  • 1 tbsp. of flour
  • 1 tbsp. of oregano
  • 3 tbsp. of olive oil
  • 4 tsp. of powdered garlic
  • 4 tsp. of powdered onion
  • 2 tsp. of salt
  • 1 tsp. of powdered black pepper
  • 1 cup of chopped onion
  • 1 cup of cuscus
  • 2 cups of chopped mushrooms
  • 1 cup of lemon juice
  • 200 ml of white wine
  • 300 ml of semi-skimmed milk
  • 300 ml of water

Directions

  1. Heat up a big pan and add the olive oil, the powdered garlic, powdered onion, oregano, black pepper, salt, the chopped onions and the mushrooms. Cook until it the onions and mushrooms become soft.
  2. Take away the mushrooms and onions to a different pot.
  3. Place the tuna steaks in the pan (without washing the pan), add the lemon juice and the white wine, cook each side for about 6 minutes, then turnoff the stove.
  4. In the pot with the mushrooms and onions add the milk, flour and 1 tbsp. of butter. Stir for 8 minutes until it becomes a sauce.
  5. in a small pot put the cuscus (do not put over fire), one pinch of salt, add the water and 1 tbsp. of butter, stir until the butter is melted and close the pot with a lid
  6. After 5 minutes the cuscus will be ready and you can serve the tuna steak with mushroom sauce and cuscus.
